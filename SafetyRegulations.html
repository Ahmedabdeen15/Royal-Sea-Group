Our Safety Regulations

Royal Sea Group’s full commitment to safety is exemplified through our safety culture and our dedication to our people.

We work tirelessly to continually develop and enhance the safety principles that underpin all of our operations. Our aim is to cultivate a safety culture and ingrained safety behaviors that surpass any regulatory requirements. We regularly assess and update our safety management system, worker and supervisor training, and worker engagement programs to align with the needs of our workforce and the industry.

Our safety philosophy at Royal Sea Group is to transform our “can do” approach into “can do safely,” all while achieving outstanding results. All work is conducted in strict accordance with applicable legislation and regulations, including Egyptian Law 12/2003 for labor and Egyptian Environmental Law 4/1994.

In the event of incidents that may occur despite our best efforts, we will ensure swift responses to address the issues at their root causes in a transparent and positive manner.
